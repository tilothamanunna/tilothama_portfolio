01 — About

From Mainframes to Modern Analytics

I'm a Graduate Information Systems student at the University of Memphis with a background that bridges enterprise mainframe development and modern data analytics. At Capgemini, I spent nearly 3 years as a Mainframe Developer building and enhancing payment module functionalities for ANZ Bank.

That experience gave me something most data analysts don't have: a deep understanding of how financial data is generated, structured, and governed at the source — inside the systems that actually run global payments.

My research project CystaSense — a comprehensive Python health analytics platform for Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS) clinical data analysis — was presented at the Fogelman Research Colloquium. This platform processes multi-variable health datasets to generate personalized risk profiles and treatment insights.
